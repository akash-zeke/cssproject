Shortly About Mario

Super Mario is a platform game series created by Nintendo based on Mario. Alternatively called the Super Mario Bros.

The SM games are in the fictional Mushroom Kingdom with Mario as the player character. He is often joined by his brother, Luigi.The game plot typically deals with Mario rescuing the kidnapped Princess Peach.
